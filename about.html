About

Jim Clauss Remodeling consistently provides top-notch craftsmanship in home renovations. Our expertise includes bathroom/kitchen remodeling, additions, basement renovations, and more! As a third generation builder, Jim has maintained the highest level of honesty and integrity when it comes to remodeling homes.

As a professional contractor servicing the Montgomery & Bucks County areas for over 25 years, Jim enjoys every part of the home renovation process. From bathroom renovations to adding energy saving features, such as new windows and doors; there is no project too big or too small.

Clients know that they will be getting the best level of service available in a general contractor, at a rate that will not break the bank. Jim Clauss Remodeling will help you decide how to make the most of your money, so that you can add value to your home, as well as customize it to your growing needs.

We look forward to seeing how we can help you design, build or add to your home!
